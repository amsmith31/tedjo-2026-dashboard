HOW IT WORKS

Three steps, zero guesswork

1

Ingest

ROMULUS pulls the complete public record — council minutes, votes, news, video and the political-ad library — the moment it exists.

2

Verify

Every claim is cross-checked against the primary source and ranked by strength. Nothing is invented; unverifiable rumours are flagged, not repeated.

3

Deploy

You get ranked OPPO, your affirmative case and a grounded AI analyst — all sourced and ready for an ad, a mailer or a debate.
